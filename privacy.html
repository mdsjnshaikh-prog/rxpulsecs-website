Privacy Policy

Privacy for doctors and patient records matters.

This page explains how the public RxPulse website handles account-related information, security checks, and support contact details. Patient records are handled inside the app and backend, not on public signup pages.

Information we collect

For public signup, RxPulse collects only the details needed to create a secure login account. BMDC, degree, specialty, and other professional profile details are collected inside the app during the approval request.

Security controls

Signup, verification, and password reset flows use time-limited links and protective checks to reduce abuse. Doctors should use the latest email link and avoid sharing account credentials.

Patient data

Patient data is handled inside the RxPulse app and backend under doctor-specific access rules. The public website is used for account and support workflows and does not collect patient clinical records.
