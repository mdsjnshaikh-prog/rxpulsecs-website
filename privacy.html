Privacy Policy

How RxPulse handles information on this website.

This page explains what information is collected on the RxPulse public website. Patient records are kept in RxPulse and are not collected on the public signup page.

Information we collect

For website signup, RxPulse asks only for the information needed to create a doctor account. BMDC, degree, specialty, and chamber details are added later in RxPulse before approval.

Account safety

Doctors should use their own email, keep the password private, and use only the newest email sent by RxPulse for confirmation or password reset.

Patient records

Patient records are used inside RxPulse for doctor work. The public website is only for account creation, password help, download information, and support.
