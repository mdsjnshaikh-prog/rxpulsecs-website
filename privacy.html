Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how RxPulse Clinician Suite handles information related to doctors, accounts, subscriptions, support, and software usage.

Last updated: 2026

1. About RxPulse

RxPulse Clinician Suite is being developed as an offline-first EMR and prescription-writing software for Bangladeshi doctors. The public website is used for product information, download, support, pricing, and policy pages.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on the feature used, RxPulse may collect or process:

  • Doctor name and professional information
  • Registered email address and phone number
  • Doctor ID and account approval status
  • Subscription plan and payment verification information
  • Support messages sent to RxPulse
  • Basic technical information needed for login, sync, backup, restore, and security

3. Patient and Prescription Data

RxPulse is intended to handle sensitive patient and prescription data. Patient data should never be made public through the website. In the final desktop software, patient and prescription data should be saved locally first and synced to cloud services only according to the software rules, doctor account status, and subscription settings.

4. Offline and Local Data

RxPulse is designed to support offline-first doctor workflow. Some data may be stored locally on the doctor’s computer so chamber work can continue during unstable internet. Doctors should keep their device secure and avoid sharing access to patient data.

5. Cloud Sync, Backup, and Restore

Online features such as account approval, subscription verification, cloud sync, backup, restore, and password reset may require internet access and cloud services such as Supabase or other trusted providers.

6. Email Communication

RxPulse may send emails for password reset, account approval/rejection, subscription updates, support replies, and important service notifications. System emails may be sent from an official RxPulse email address such as noreply@rxpulsecs.com.

7. Data Sharing

RxPulse does not intend to sell doctor or patient data. Information may be processed through necessary service providers for authentication, hosting, email delivery, payment verification, cloud storage, sync, and support.

8. Security

RxPulse aims to use reasonable security practices. However, no software or internet service can guarantee absolute security. Doctors are responsible for keeping their login credentials and devices safe.

9. Doctor Responsibilities

Doctors are responsible for using RxPulse ethically, protecting patient confidentiality, and following applicable medical, professional, and legal responsibilities in Bangladesh.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as RxPulse develops new features or improves its service. Updated versions may be published on this website.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@rxpulsecs.com.
