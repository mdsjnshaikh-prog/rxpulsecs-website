How to update

Using the WebApp? Just refresh when an update appears.

RxPulse WebApp runs in the browser. When a new WebApp update is available, a message will appear on the Dashboard. Save or sync ongoing work, then refresh the app.

1

Check the Dashboard notice

If a new update is available, the doctor Dashboard will show an update notice.

2

Save or sync your work

Before refreshing, finish current prescriptions, patient records, or any unsynced work.

3

Refresh the app

WebApp updates usually do not need an installer download. Refreshing the app loads the new version.

If an update does not load

If the internet is weak, the update notice or latest WebApp version may take a little time to load. To avoid risk to ongoing prescriptions or patient data, save your work first, then refresh or reopen the app. If the problem continues, contact support@rxpulsecs.com.
