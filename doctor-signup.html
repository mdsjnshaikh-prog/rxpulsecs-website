Doctor signup

Verify your email first

Before creating a RxPulse doctor account, your email must be verified. No account, password, or doctor profile is created before email verification.

1Enter email

Use an email you can open now. We do not ask for a password at this step.

2Open the link within 5 minutes

The email verification link is valid for 5 minutes. Please check your inbox and spam folder.

3Set password

After opening the verification link, set your password. Your account is created only then.

Secure signup rule

RxPulse does not create an Auth user, app user, or doctor profile before email verification.
