Terms

Terms for using the RxPulse website and doctor account.

These terms explain the basic rules for creating an account, confirming email, sending professional details for review, and using RxPulse responsibly.

Doctor account

Website signup creates a doctor account. Full use of RxPulse requires email confirmation, professional information, and approval.

Approval

Doctors should provide correct BMDC, degree, specialty, and chamber information. RxPulse may approve the account, ask for correction, or stop account use if information is not appropriate.

Using RxPulse

RxPulse helps with prescription writing, patient records, medicine support, and printing. It does not replace the doctor’s clinical judgment, patient discussion, legal responsibility, or professional duty.
