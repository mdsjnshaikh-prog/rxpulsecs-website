Terms

Terms for using RxPulse public website and account services.

These terms summarize how doctors should use RxPulse public account pages, verification links, approval requests, and software access. The in-app clinical workflow remains subject to professional responsibility and RxPulse policy.

Doctor account

Public signup creates a login account only. Clinical use requires email verification, professional profile completion inside the app, and Admin approval before access is enabled.

Approval

Doctors must submit accurate professional and chamber information inside the app. Admin may approve, approve with trial, reject, disable, or request correction according to account status and RxPulse policy.

Software use

RxPulse supports prescription writing, EMR records, reference guidance, and print workflow. It does not replace the doctor’s clinical judgment, patient communication, legal responsibility, or local professional obligations.
