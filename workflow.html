How it works

How doctors start using RxPulse in simple steps.

A doctor first enters an email, opens the verification link, sets a password, then signs in to RxPulse and sends professional details for review. RxPulse can be used after approval.

1

Enter email

Use an email you can open now. No password is taken at this step.

2

Open verification link

Open the verification link sent to your email within the allowed time. Check both inbox and spam folder.

3

Set password

After opening the verification link, set your password. Your account is created then.

4

Sign in to RxPulse

Sign in to RxPulse with your new password.

5

Add professional details

Add BMDC number, professional name, degree, specialty, chamber information, and other required details.

6

Send for review

Send the completed information for review. After approval, the doctor can start using the software for chamber work.
