How it works

How doctors start using RxPulse in simple steps.

A doctor first creates an account and confirms the email. Then the doctor adds professional details, sends them for review, and starts using RxPulse after approval.

1

Create account

Start with your email address and a password. Professional information is added later so the first step stays simple.

2

Confirm your email

Open the confirmation email and complete the email check soon. This helps make sure the account belongs to you.

3

Open RxPulse

Open RxPulse and use the same email and password you used during signup.

4

Add professional details

Add BMDC number, professional name, degree, specialty, chamber information, and other required details.

5

Send for review

Send the completed information for review. You can then wait for approval before using all doctor features.

6

Approval from RxPulse

RxPulse reviews the information and updates the account. After approval, the doctor can start using the software for chamber work.
