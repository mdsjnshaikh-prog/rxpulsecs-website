Features

Helpful features for prescription writing, patient records, and daily chamber work.

RxPulse follows a normal patient visit: select or add a patient, note vital signs and findings, write medicines and advice, save the visit, and print a clear prescription. It helps doctors work faster while keeping patient information easier to find later.

01

Prescription writing

Write prescriptions in a guided order: patient details, vital signs, findings, diagnosis, medicines, advice, and print choice. The layout helps reduce repeated typing and missed sections.

02

Patient records

Patient profiles, previous prescriptions, and follow-up notes stay together. Return visits become easier because important history is available in one place.

03

Works when internet is weak

In many chambers, internet service can be unstable. RxPulse is designed so doctors can continue important work and update records when the connection becomes available.

04

Medicine support

Medicine suggestions, dosage form labels, interaction warnings, and patient advice notes are kept close to the prescription area to support safer and more consistent prescribing.

05

Clean printed prescription

Prepare a clean prescription for patients with options for clinic pad use, doctor or chamber header, and readable formatting.

06

Approval and use plan

New doctors send their professional information for review. After approval, they can start using RxPulse according to the plan selected for their account.
