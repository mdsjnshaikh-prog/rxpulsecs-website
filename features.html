Features

Core tools for daily prescription and EMR work.

RxPulse focuses on the work doctors repeat every day: writing prescriptions, reviewing patient history, preparing print-ready output, and keeping records usable even when the internet is unreliable.

01

Prescription editor

Patient information, vitals, examination, diagnosis notes, medicines, advice, and print settings stay in one guided flow so each prescription can be completed with fewer clicks.

02

Patient and EMR records

Patient profiles and previous prescriptions remain connected, making follow-up visits easier to review and reducing repeated writing for return patients.

03

Offline-friendly work

Local-first behavior supports chamber work during unstable internet. Doctors can continue essential tasks and sync records when the connection becomes available.

04

Reference guidance

Medicine suggestions, dosage-form labels, adverse-effect notes, and interaction warnings are organized to support safer and more consistent prescribing decisions.

05

Print-ready output

Clean prescription output supports clinic pads, doctor/chamber header preferences, and practical patient handover after the consultation.

06

Admin approval and plans

Doctors submit their professional profile from inside the app. Admin can review approval, trial, subscription, and account status from a controlled workflow.
